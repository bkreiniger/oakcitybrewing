Oak City Brewing Co.

A local craft and specialty brewery located in the heart of Raleigh, NC. We focus on small batch microbrews with an intense attention to detial.

About us

Local, Organic, Small Batch Brewing

Simply put - we aim to create wholesome, delicious and authentic beer to satisify the most discerning of pallets.

This is why we use only the highest quality, local, organic ingredients and keep our batch size as small as possible to more thoroughly control the quality of our product.

Our Mission:

Quality, not Quantity

We believe in the power of focus, and in perfecting our craft as finely as possible.

Each of our brews is honed over years of trial and error. We hyperfocus on the most minute of details to create an unparalled experience and enjoyment. We also offer a gastropub food pairing experience to multiply the experience of your pallete.
